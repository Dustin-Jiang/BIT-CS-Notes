# $RLC$ 元件 VCR 相量形式

## 电容

$$ I_m = \omega CU_m $$
电流提前电压的角度为 $90\degree$: 
$$ \phi_i = \phi_u + {90}\degree $$
$$ \dot I_m = j\omega C \dot U_m $$

## 电感

$$ U_m = \omega LI_m $$
电流滞后电压的角度为 $90\degree$: 
$$ \phi_u = \phi_i + {90}\degree $$
$$ \dot U_m = j\omega L \dot I_m $$

# 阻抗

电容阻抗 $Z = \frac 1{j\omega C}$; 电阻阻抗 $Z=j\omega L$.
